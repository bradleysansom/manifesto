A manifesto for responsive identity design

By Bradley Sansom

This project originated in my Design Futures unit. I began to think about what led me to being here: what brought me to the world of graphic design. What even is ‘graphic design’? And why do I have a degree in it? I spent a while pondering those questions, and started to figure out my design origin story.

Because I was never really comfortable with ‘art’. Even when I was a kid, when we’d get the paints out at school, I’d be the one sat in the corner designing a system of wayfinding signs. Always a designer, never an artist. On one occasion, I’d bedecked the classroom like it was a small branch of Asda, complete with ample ‘no smoking’ signs.
